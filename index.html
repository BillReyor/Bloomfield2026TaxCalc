Bloomfield Tax Calculator (FY 2025–FY 2029)

Enter your property’s post-revaluation assessed value (the 70% CT assessed figure, effective FY 2026), along with either your old (pre-reval) assessed value or your Revaluation Increase (%). We need one of those to calculate the four-year 25% phase-in correctly. If you leave both blank, we can’t produce an accurate phase-in comparison.
