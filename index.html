Bloomfield Tax Calculator (FY 2025–FY 2029)

Enter your property’s post-revaluation assessed value (effective FY 2026), along with either your old (pre-reval) assessed value or your Revaluation Increase (%). We need one of those two to calculate the four-year phase-in correctly. If you leave both blank, you’ll see a warning and no tax figures.

Assessed Values

The fully revalued assessment (70 % of market value). Do not enter a 25 %-phased number here—this must be the 100 % revalued figure.

(Optional) If you know your old assessment before October 2024, enter it here.

― or ―

(Optional) If your notice says “New assessment is 55 % higher,” enter 0.55. We’ll back out your old assessed value from that.

These show what the mill would be if all properties were already fully assessed at 70 % of market value. In other words, they are the “revenue-neutral” base rate for each phase-in level. (They are not used directly to calculate your tax—only Council mills are used for the final tax.)
